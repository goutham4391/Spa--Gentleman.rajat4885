Owner of Gentleman's Barber Spa in White Plains with Former Knicks Great John Starks

January 28, 2016/ Peter Palushi

Gentleman's Barber Spa creator, Peter Palushi, spent Sunday, January 24th - Tuesday, January 26th at the Javits Center in NYC for MRket. He was asked by former employer and royal barber, Truefitt & Hill of London, to perform the Royal Shave for MRket Show attendees.

One of the gentleman Peter came across during his work at MRket was former Knicks shooting guard, John Starks. There's just something about a classic barber shop, a relaxing shave, a trim and a place to kick back. That's why guys like John Starks, actor Sean Bean and many other public figures like Gentleman's Barber Spa. 

 

Peter displayed the skill and technique of a master barber while representing the Truefitt & Hill  brand, history and grooming products. Stay tuned for more news about Truefitt & Hill products, as well as more Gentleman's Barber Spa news, notes and style tips.
